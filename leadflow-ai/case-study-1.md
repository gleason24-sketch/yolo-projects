# Case Study #1: ABC Plumbing

## Client Profile
- **Company:** ABC Plumbing
- **Location:** Denver, CO
- **Industry:** Plumbing (Trade Services)
- **Size:** 8 employees
- **Revenue:** $1.2M/year

---

## The Problem

ABC Plumbing was spending $2,400/month on Google Ads with poor results:
- 20 leads/month (mostly bad quality)
- $120 cost per lead
- 5+ hours/week owner doing sales
- Missed calls, lost leads

**Total monthly spend:** $2,400 (ads) + $1,000 (owner time) = **$3,400/month**

---

## The Solution

LeadFlow AI implemented:

### Month 1:
- Set up targeting for Denver metro area
- Created personalized outreach for plumbing industry
- Connected to owner's calendar
- Sent 150 personalized emails

### Month 2:
- Optimized based on response data
- A/B tested subject lines
- Added LinkedIn research
- Scaled to 200 emails/week

---

## Results (After 90 Days)

### Lead Generation
- **Before:** 20 leads/month
- **After:** 85 qualified leads/month
- **Improvement:** 325% increase

### Cost Per Lead
- **Before:** $120/lead
- **After:** $18/lead
- **Improvement:** 85% reduction

### Revenue Impact
- 12 new customers from LeadFlow leads
- Average job: $1,800
- **New revenue:** $21,600
- **ROI:** 18x investment

### Time Savings
- Owner time: 5 hours/week → 1 hour/week
- Value of time saved: $800/month
