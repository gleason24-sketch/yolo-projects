# LeadFlow AI - OPERATIONS MANUAL

## Where Leads Go

### Lead Flow
1. Lead submits form on website
2. Lead saved to CRM (Airtable/Notion)
3. Automated email to lead confirming receipt
4. Human (Scott) reviews lead
5. Schedule demo call
6. Close/sale

### CRM Structure

**Fields:**
- Name
- Email
- Phone
- Company
- Industry
- Budget
- Notes
- Plan Interest
- Status: New → Contacted → Demo Scheduled → Proposal → Won/Lost
- Follow-up Date
- Assigned To

### Notification
- New lead → Discord notification to Scott
- Daily lead digest at 9am CST

## Payment Processing

### Options

**Option A: Stripe (Recommended)**
- Easy integration
- Recurring payments
- Invoice generation
- Payment links

**Option B: PayPal**
- Alternative for hesitant customers
- Easier for older business owners

**Option C: Manual Invoice**
- Send invoice via email
- Bank transfer
- Cash/check for local

### Subscription Flow
1. Customer signs up
2. Stripe invoice sent
3. Payment collected automatically
4. Access granted on payment
5. Monthly recurring

## Marketing Plan (No Ad Spend)

### Phase 1: Content & Authority (Week 1-4)

**Daily Actions:**
- Post 3x/day on LinkedIn + X
- Share case studies
- Behind-the-scenes content

**Content Types:**
1. Case study posts (before/after)
2. "A client made $X" posts
3. Problem-solution content
4. Testimonial shares
5. Industry news commentary

**Hashtags:**
#LeadGeneration #SmallBusiness #Entrepreneur #HVAC #Plumbing #ServiceBusiness #Marketing

### Phase 2: Direct Outreach (Week 3-8)

**Target:** 50 businesses/day

**Process:**
1. Find businesses in target industries (use our tools)
2. Identify decision maker on LinkedIn
3. Personalized connection request
4. Follow up with value
5. Demo request

**Script:**
> "Hey [Name], noticed [Company] doing [Industry] work in [Location]. We've been helping similar companies generate 3-5x more leads. Would you be open to a quick 10-min call to show you what's working?"

### Phase 3: Referrals (Week 4+)

**Incentives:**
- 1 month free for each referral
- Discount for multi-location

**Process:**
- Ask every client for referrals
- Partner with complementary businesses
- HVAC → roofers → plumbers

### Phase 4: Partnerships (Ongoing)

**Partners:**
- Industry associations
- Business groups (BNI, etc.)
- Complementary service providers
- Chamber of Commerce
